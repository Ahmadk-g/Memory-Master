Rules of the Game


Welcome to Memory Master! Get ready to excercise your brain and challenge your memory skills.



  1. Gameplay

    Click on any two cards to reveal their hidden symbols. If the symbols match, the cards stay face-up. If not, they'll flip back over, so stay focused.

  2. Scoring

    • Gradual Mode:

      Race against the clock, match all pairs across three levels of varying difficulty, aiming for a record-breaking time.

    • Difficulty Mode:

      Maintain an unbroken streak of victories at your chosen difficulty level and set a new record for a win streak.
